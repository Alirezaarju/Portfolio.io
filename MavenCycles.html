Project Objective:

This is a guided project by Aaron Parry from Maven Analytics via E-learning platform Udemy.com, The goal is to use the entire Microsoft Power BI ecosystem to design and deploy reports and dashboards to share across the organization. In order to accomplish this there are few objectives:
1. Prepare data
2. Model The Data
3. Visualize the data
4. Analyze the data
5. Deploy and maintain the deliverables

Project Description:

3. Actionable Insights: Asked to build end-to-end business intelligence solution from the ground up,using raw data containing information about sales, products, customers and store location

Insights Drawn:

1. The total sale from year 2012 till 2015 is 12.64 M, the toral revenue is 986.57M and the total quantity sold is 178K.
2. The average delivery time is 4 days, and the total returned products are 1079.
3. Canon Image class is the highest profitable product.
3. The top 5 Customers data is very similar to each other.
4. Consumer Segment holds 51.48%, Corporate segment is 30.25% and Home Office 18.27% of sales.
5. there are several loss-making products which needs to be sold quickly and be careful regarding purchasing/manufacturing them in the future.
