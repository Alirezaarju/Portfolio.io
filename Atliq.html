This project is my personal project where I have created Interactive Hardware sales report using Microsoft Powerbi. Here I have followed some steps such as:

1. Connecting DB
2. Analyzing Tables & Relations
3. Data Cleaning - Power Query Editor, DAX
4. Developing Model
5. Creating Dashboard.


Project Objective:

Track the sales of dynamically growing market of computer hardware and peripherals.
I would like to thank Dhaval Patel and his youtube channel code basics for the awesome project idea and providing the data set.

Project Description:

The objective can be broken down into the following detailed component.
1. Dashboard creation: Identify the KPIâ€™s, design an intuitive and visually appearing dashboard, and interactive visualization and filtering capability to allow users to explore the data at various levels of granularity.
2. Data analysis: Provide valuable insights regarding the hardware sales.
3. Actionable Insights: The goal is to provide the real scenario of the business, comparison of sales quarter to quarter. With the help of this dashboard stakeholders can find out which region is more profitable, which regional manage is active in his job, what precautions can be taken to increase the sales and business development.

Insights Drawn:

1. from year 2018 till 2020 the toral revenue is 986.57M and the total quantity sold is 2M.
2. Year 2018 is most profitable among all other, the company made revenue of 414.31 M and sold 998K products
3. Compared to the year 2018, the sales declined drastically. In 2019 total revenue was 336.45M whereas in 2020 its just 142.24M with that Delhi being the top market is lost a revenue of nearly 100M within a year.
4. 70.01% of the total sales came from North zone of India. Central zone brings 25.38% sales and South zone brings 4.61% sales.
5. Among top 5 customers, 4 customers are below 10M of sales figure. Electricalsara stores alone generated a sale of 65M.
6. There are several loss-making products which needs to be sold even in lower price than purchasing price/manufacturing cost and be careful regarding purchasing/manufacturing them in the future.
