Project Objective:

Draw the insight from call log data downloaded from VoIP App, My client presented the visualization to his company client to grab a business project.

Project Description:

The objective can be broken down into the following detailed component.
1. Dashboard creation: Identify the KPIâ€™s, design an intuitive and visually appearing dashboard, and interactive visualization and filtering capability to allow users to explore the data at various levels of granularity.
2. Data analysis: Provide valuable insights regarding the superstore sales.
3. Actionable Insights: The goal is to provide the real scenario of the call log, comparison of Totals calls handled. With the help of this dashboard stakeholders can find out the most busiest day of the week, how many calls are handled in a month

Insights Drawn:

1. The total number of calls were 2737 among them 1329 calls are inbound calls and 1408 calls are outbound calls.
2. The average calls per day is 23.73.
3. From 8am till 3pm number of calls decreases gradually.
3. The sum of calls handled on monday is 660, tuesday is 450, wednesday is wed 546, thursday is 589, friday is 464.
4. 1% of total calls were handled on weekends
5. Second week of every month got the heighest percentage of calls.
